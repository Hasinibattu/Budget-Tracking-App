Frequently Asked Questions

What is a budget tracker?
A budget tracker is a tool that helps you plan, manage, and track your income and expenses to better control your personal finances.
Is my financial data safe?
Yes, we prioritize your privacy. All data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent.
How do I add a new expense?
Navigate to the dashboard, click on "Add Expense", fill out the details, and click "Save". You can categorize expenses for better tracking.
Can I track multiple budgets?
Yes, you can create and manage multiple budgets for different purposes like groceries, travel, bills, and more.
Is this service free?
Yes! Our core features are completely free. We may offer premium features in the future, but basic tracking will always be free.
Can I export my budget or reports?
As of now, you cannot export your budget or reports. However, we are working on this feature for future updates.
How do I reset my account or data?
Go to Settings > Account Management. You’ll find options to reset your data or delete your account entirely.
